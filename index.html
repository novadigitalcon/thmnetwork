About us

The Haven Millennials Networks (THMN) consist of different platforms of expressions for millennials between the ages of 18 to 30 years who are committed to the evangelization of the world through Kingdom Financing and Leadership / Capacity Development.

We are young, passionate, easily-persuaded, very teachable and poised Finance Ministers who are all about recruiting like-ages and converting them to like-minds, in a bid to build the work force that is required in these last days to take the world

MUTUAL ACCOUNTABILITY

LIQUID LOVE

NETWORKS & ACHIEVEMENTS

THMN Business Development Network

This network has inspired over one hundred and fifty (150) millennials to found new businesses across fifteen (15) industries in twenty (20) countries of which all of the founding millennials channel specific percentage of their profits into giving for Haven projects.
Subscribe Now

THMN Leadership Development Network

This team has trained over three thousand (3,000) millennial in self and people leadership, starting and growing businesses, expanding kingdom finance, life coaching and skill acquisition through different developmental programs and projects across 21 countries. Within The Haven Nation, this network has been responsible for pioneering over one hundred (100) Friendship Circles and forty (40) professional millennial cells across twelve (12) countries including Canada, Namibia, Nigeria, South Africa, Uganda, UK, USA, Zambia, Zimbabwe, and more.

THMN Kingdom Culture Network

This network has been responsible for instituting the #ProjectAMessageADay where millennials listened to word-based messages from our Man of God, Pastor Chris every day; thereby imbibing in them, the God-mindset – a mindset of creativity, innovation, possibilities, and excellence. This network was also responsible for initiating the #SayIt1000TimesADayChallenge that has instilled in us the practice and culture of talking right every day.
